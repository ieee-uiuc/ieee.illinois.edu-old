Welcome to the IEEE UIUC Projects Fair

IEEE is hosting a projects fair where groups and RSOs with technical projects can showcase an idea to start a new project or progress on existing projects that need help. It will be an open event held at ECE Building main lobby (Atrium). Check us out on your way to classes! Talk to project teams on campus! Be a part of our UIUC's projects community!

RSOs!

This is the best opportunity to publicize your organization, share your passion, and more importantly, tell people about your ideas and what you are doing.

Students!

If you are interested in startups teams, have your own startup ideas, or want to get your own team, come to the event! Talk to project organizations, enjoy an afternoon with these innovative minds, and get involved!
