About

28th Annual Midwestern Robotics Design Competition (MRDC)

We are a robotics group at the University of Illinois - Urbana Champaign that competes annually at the MRDC. We aim to further engineering knowledge and develop applied real life design and development skills through collaborative robotics engineering.

Engineering students from around the United States design and build robots to compete in a task-based goal. Each year, a new objective is created that tests the skills of the teams in creative design and functionality. After almost one year of designing and constructing their robot for the event, students from different universities nationwide travel to the UIUC Engineering Open House in March for two days to compete in the 2,000 sq. ft. arena. The event attracts thousands of spectators and we are actively recruiting new members for 2016. Don't be shy!
